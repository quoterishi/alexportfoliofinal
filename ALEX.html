ABOUT ME

Hi I am Alex The web designer ,I work with many small businesses to create websites that help them stand out from their competitors. My favorite websites focus on simplicity and use a lot of white space, creative copy, and beautiful graphics. You can see some examples of the work I have done on my portfolio page. A brief background on my upbringing: My parents own a small business and I learned a lot about business from them. I learned to think about business. This knowledge base is very important. It gives me the tools to understand the business behind the websites I create and helps me see opportunities that others may not. I've never created a website before. Before I started web design, my life was fun. For over three years, I've taught ESL in Korea, picked apples in New Zealand, traded agricultural products and currencies, observed new products, and traveled the world. I continue to benefit from these experiences in my work and life.
